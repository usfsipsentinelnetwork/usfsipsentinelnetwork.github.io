Impacts

impact_time dimension impact_type host impact
1 future species Susceptibility to other stressors Acer spp. In the pacific northwest, where summer droughts are increasing in severity, susceptible exotic host trees (e.g., Acer pseudoplatanus) will be killed off in urban areas and removed from planting lists. As drought severity increases we may see impacts on endemic maple species (e.g., A. macrophyllum, A. circinatum) depending on the pathogenicity of C. corticale to these species.
